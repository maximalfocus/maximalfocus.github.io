### Starlink in India: The Physics of Connectivity

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    Start[Internet Connectivity Needs] --> Decision{Population Density?}

    subgraph Rural ["Low Density Zone (Remote)"]
        direction TB
        Starlink[Starlink / Satellite]
        Starlink -->|Reasoning| D1[Physics: Signal travels faster in vacuum]
        Starlink -->|Economics| D2[Cheaper than laying physical cables]
    end

    subgraph Urban ["High Density Zone (Cities)"]
        direction TB
        Fiber[Fiber Optics / 5G]
        Fiber -->|Reasoning| C1[Physics: Short distance, high capacity]
        Fiber -->|Economics| C2[Cost shared by millions in small area]
    end

    Decision -- High --> Fiber
    Decision -- Low --> Starlink
    
    style Fiber fill:#fff4dd,stroke:#d4a017
    style Starlink fill:#e1f5fe,stroke:#0277bd
    style Decision fill:#f9f,stroke:#333
```
